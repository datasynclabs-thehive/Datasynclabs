# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is this an AI model company?
No. It is a coordination and incentive infrastructure for AI systems.

## Do you have a token?
Not yet. Tokenomics are conceptual and simulated only.

## Can I contribute without coding?
Yes. Many critical components are non-technical.

## Is this open source?
Yes, with governance and validation.

## Why not build first?
Because coordination failures are expensive to fix later.
